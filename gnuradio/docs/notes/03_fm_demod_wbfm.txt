✅ What you need to adjust first

Only one variable:
f_station
This is the transmitter frequency in Hz. Example:
100.3 MHz → 100300000
95.0 MHz → 95000000
89.0 MHz → 89000000
Troubleshooting (if you can't hear anything):
Vary manual_gain1 to 40–70
Make sure your audio output device works in Linux
If the FM transmitter is very strong: Lower the gain
If you don't see any signal at all: Check the antenna/frequency/Pluto URI
