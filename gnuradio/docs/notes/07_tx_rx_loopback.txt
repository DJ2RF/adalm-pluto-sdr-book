Here's how to test it safely (briefly):

First, increase TX_atten: tx_atten = 40 (less power)

Keep tx_amp low: 0.02 … 0.05

Try RX Gain 20…50

You should see a clear peak in the RX spectrum at f0 ± tone_hz (depending on the display, this peak will be "relative" around the center).
